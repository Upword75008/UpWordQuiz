What do these common idioms mean?

1. "It's raining cats and dogs"

✅ Correct answer: B) It's raining very heavily

2. "Break a leg!"

✅ Correct answer: B) Good luck!

3. "Spill the beans"

✅ Correct answer: B) Reveal a secret

4. "Hit the books"

✅ Correct answer: B) Start studying hard

5. "Under the weather"

✅ Correct answer: A) Feeling sick
