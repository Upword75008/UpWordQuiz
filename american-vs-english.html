American vs British English

Match the American word with its British equivalent.

1. Elevator = ?

A) Escalator
B) Lift
C) Stairs
Correct answer: B) Lift

2. Cookie = ?

A) Biscuit
B) Cracker
C) Cake
Correct answer: A) Biscuit

3. Vacation = ?

A) Holiday
B) Break
C) Weekend
Correct answer: A) Holiday

4. Trash can = ?

A) Rubbish bin
B) Waste basket
C) Garbage bag
Correct answer: A) Rubbish bin

5. Apartment = ?

A) House
B) Flat
C) Room
Correct answer: B) Flat
